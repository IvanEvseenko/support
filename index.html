Privacy Policy for Offline Talk App

Effective Date: January 5, 2025

Offline Talk App is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data and ensures transparency about our practices.

Data Collection

Offline Talk App does not collect, store, or share any personal or device data. All features of the app operate fully offline, utilizing Bluetooth technology for communication.

What This Means

  • No Data Transmission: Your messages and other interactions are processed locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your usage, location, or any other activities.
  • No Third-Party Services: The app does not integrate with analytics or advertising services that collect user data.

Data Security

Since Offline Talk App does not collect or store data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed. Bluetooth communication is secured locally between devices.

Children's Privacy

Offline Talk App is suitable for users of all ages. No personal information is collected from children or any other users.
